Our Story

The church has been in journey to maturity to look like Christ who said “I will build my church” in the transiton so much has to be lost and much has been restored, God is reforming the church in our day to bring her to the original mandate that He purposed in the beginning.

We believe in the power of faith to transform lives and communities. Through worship, fellowship, and service, we strive to be a beacon of hope and a source of strength for all who seek spiritual nourishment.
