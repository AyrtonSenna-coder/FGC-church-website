Featured Ministry

Children's Ministry

Ages 3-12 Sundays 9:00 AM & 11:00 AM Children's Wing

Our Children's Ministry provides a safe, engaging environment where kids can learn about God's love through age-appropriate lessons, activities, and worship. We're committed to partnering with parents to nurture children's spiritual growth.

Each week, our dedicated team of volunteers creates memorable experiences that help children build a foundation of faith that will last a lifetime.
