Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What should I expect when I visit for the first time?

When you visit Fountain Gate Church for the first time, you can expect a warm welcome from our greeters, uplifting worship music, and a relevant Bible-based message. Our services typically last about 75 minutes. Feel free to dress comfortably - you'll see everything from casual to business attire.

Do you have programs for children and youth?

Yes! We have age-appropriate programs for children from nursery through high school. Our Children's Ministry provides engaging lessons and activities during both Sunday services. Our Youth Group meets Friday evenings for fellowship, games, and Bible study. All our volunteers are background-checked and trained to provide a safe environment.

How can I get involved in serving at the church?

There are many opportunities to serve at Grace Community Church! You can join our greeting team, help with children's ministry, participate in our outreach programs, assist with worship, or help with facilities. We recommend starting by attending our "Discover Grace" class, which provides an overview of our church and ways to get connected.

Do you offer counseling services?

Yes, we offer biblical counseling for individuals, couples, and families. Our pastoral staff provides counseling for various life issues including marriage, grief, addiction, and spiritual growth. To schedule an appointment, please contact the church office. There may be a waiting list for non-emergency counseling.

How can I request prayer?

You can request prayer in several ways: submit a prayer request through our website contact form, email prayer@gracecommunitychurch.org, call the church office, or fill out a prayer request card during our services. Our prayer team prays over all requests confidentially. For urgent prayer needs, you can call the church office during business hours.
