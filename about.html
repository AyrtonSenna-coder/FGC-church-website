About Our Church

Discover our story, mission, and the people who make Fountain Gate Church - Utawala a spiritual home for so many.

Our Story

Fountain Gate Church - Utawala was founded in 2020 by Pastor Morris and Jemimah Ochieng' with a vision to create a welcoming spiritual home for people from all walks of life. What began as a small Bible study in their living room has grown into a vibrant community of faith serving hundreds of families in our area.

Over the years, we've remained committed to our founding principles of grace, compassion, and service. Our doors are open to everyone seeking spiritual growth, meaningful relationships, and opportunities to make a difference in our community.

Today, under the leadership of Pastor Morris Ochieng', we continue to build on this legacy while adapting to meet the changing needs of our congregation and community.

Our Mission

To raise a King Priest community - by Training leaders, Equipping the Saints with the Word of God, Creating forums for equipping the body of Christ in the Apostolic Season, Raising strong families, and Mentor the youth for holistic living.

We strive to be a place where people can encounter God, experience transformation, and engage in meaningful service.

Our Vision

Fountain Gate Church CFC exists to raise a King Priest community that is committed to the Doctrine of Christ and to make Him known to the nations.

"My little children, for whom I labor in birth again until Christ is formed in you." Galatians 4:19

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God, the ultimate authority for faith and practice, and relevant for our lives today.

Salvation

We believe in salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, who died for our sins and rose again to give us eternal life.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, who empowers us for Christian living and service.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship God, care for one another, and share the Gospel with the world.

Our History

1985

Church Founding

Grace Community Church was established by Pastor John and Mary Thompson with just 12 members meeting in their home.

2020

First Building

We purchased and renovated our first church building, allowing us to expand our ministries and welcome more families.

2001

Community Outreach

Launched our community food pantry and counseling services, extending our mission beyond our church walls.

2010

New Leadership

Pastor Morris Ochieng' became our senior pastor, bringing fresh vision and energy to our growing congregation.

2018

Building Expansion

Completed a major expansion of our facilities to accommodate our growing ministries and community programs.

2025

Digital Ministry

Launched our online campus, allowing us to reach people beyond our geographical location with the message of hope.
